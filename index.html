Core dose equation (liquid-based methods)
mg_bioavailable = (vol_ml × conc_mg_per_ml) ÷ puffs_per_cart × puffs_taken × temperature_efficiency × carrier_factor × crystallization_factor × (puff_duration_factor × inhalation_style_factor) × hold_retention_factor × puff_interval_factor
Each factor reduces the theoretical maximum based on pharmacokinetic and device physics data. The largest sources of variance are temperature efficiency (device-dependent) and concentration uncertainty (especially in pre-filled or unverified cartridges).
E-mesh equation (direct freebase — no liquid)
mg_bioavailable = mg_loaded × mesh_efficiency × inhalation_factor × hold_retention
The e-mesh method bypasses the concentration/volume calculation entirely. Community data (DMT-Nexus e-mesh thread, 2019–2024) indicates 15mg loaded on SS316L 200-mesh at 190–210°C TC delivers approximately equivalent effect to 20–25mg via standard Yocan methods — consistent with ~93% efficiency vs. ~55–65% for quartz wax pen.
Temperature model
T_coil (°C) ≈ 80 + (W × 4.5) W = V² ÷ R (Ohm's Law)
Ceramic mesh coil steady-state temperature estimate. Validated qualitatively against published cannabis vaping topography data (voltage vs. coil temperature in 510-thread systems).

DMT vaporization window: 160–215°C optimal. <160°C = under-vaporization. >220°C = combustion begins.
TC/induction devices: Set temperature used directly — no estimation needed.
Ispire Daab: Induction heating into borosilicate glass cup. Only the metal within the glass walls is heated. No coil contamination, most precise portable device available.
Inhalation style factors
MTL: 0.65 (puff volume ~55ml, Behar et al. 2015) RDTL: 0.76 (intermediate) DTL: 0.88 (puff volume ~500ml, Prasad et al. 2022)
Direct-to-lung inhalation delivers ~9x the aerosol volume of mouth-to-lung per puff. This is the largest technique variable in the model. Puff duration is also a primary determinant of aerosol volume (not flow rate), per published topography research.
Uncertainty
E-mesh: ±18% — tighter because loaded mass is directly measured.
Liquid methods — known device/concentration: ±25–30%.
Liquid methods — unknown/pre-filled cart: ±35–45%. Pre-filled cart concentration is unverifiable without gas chromatography (Bunk Police documentation notes most pre-filled DMT carts are underdosed vs. advertised).

This tool produces estimated ranges for harm reduction education and survey design. It is not a clinical dosing instrument.
